# Skill: Financial Intelligence

This skill integrates global Antigravity financial capabilities into the `financial-smart-dashboard`.

## Mandates

- **Quantitative Rigor**: Always use `quant-analyst` for backtesting or modeling complex fiscal scenarios.
- **Risk Assessment**: Use `risk-metrics-calculation` to provide VaR (Value at Risk) and Sharpe Ratio analysis for the investment metrics.
- **Formulaic Integrity**: When dealing with Excel data (from `excel-connector.ts`), verify calculations against established financial formulas.

## Triggers

- "Calcular risco do portfólio"
- "Análise quantitativa de tendências"
- "Métricas Sharper/Sortino"
